International Burn Reconstruction Expert Dr. Mark Fisher Visits Linkou CGMH Burn Center

September 10, 2024 Linkou CGMH International Exchange

Prof. Mark Fisher, a renowned authority in burn and head-neck reconstruction from The Johns Hopkins Hospital, visited Linkou CGMH Burn Center for an in-depth academic exchange. The visit was accompanied by Dr. Hui-Fu Huang, President of the Taiwan Society of Burn Injury and Wound Care, marking an important milestone in Taiwan-US burn medicine cooperation.

Lecture Highlights

Free Flap Surgery Clinical Applications in Burn Reconstruction
Pediatric Burn Reconstruction Psychological Support Across Age Groups
Microsurgical Techniques Achieving Optimal Reconstruction Outcomes

Featured Lecture: Free Flap Surgery & Pediatric Burn Reconstruction

As the current Chair of the Reconstruction Committee of the American Burn Association, Dr. Fisher delivered a featured lecture covering the application of Free Flap Surgery in burn reconstruction, sharing the latest surgical techniques and clinical experience.

Ward Visits: International Recognition

Dr. Fisher visited the burn ICU and general wards, highly praising our center's quality of care. He particularly acknowledged the outstanding achievements in microsurgical flap surgery, recognizing it as a leading standard in burn care across the Asia-Pacific region.

About Dr. Mark Fisher

Dr. Mark Fisher

Dr. Mark Fisher is an internationally renowned burn reconstruction surgeon, serving as Chair of the Reconstruction Committee of the American Burn Association at Johns Hopkins Hospital. With over twenty years of experience, he specializes in complex burn scar reconstruction, free flap surgery, and head-neck reconstruction.

Future Collaboration

This visit strengthened Taiwan-US academic exchange in burn medicine and laid a solid foundation for future international collaboration. Linkou CGMH Burn Center will continue to enhance care quality and align with international standards.
