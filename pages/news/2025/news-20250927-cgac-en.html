2025 Chang Gung Aesthetics Conference (CGAC): Mastering Aesthetic Eye Surgery

September 27-28, 2025 Linkou CGMH Research Building Academic Events

Conference Overview

As aesthetic medical technology continues to advance, eyelid surgery remains one of the most demanding and technically challenging fields in Asia and worldwide. To lead the innovation in this field, the internationally renowned Linkou Chang Gung Memorial Hospital Department of Plastic Surgery announces the 14th Chang Gung Aesthetics Conference, to be held on September 27-28, 2025 at the International Conference Hall of Linkou CGMH Research Building.

This conference, themed "Mastering Aesthetic Eye Surgery", aims to explore the latest advances in periocular aesthetics and reconstructive surgery through a two-day intensive academic symposium. The conference features international masters from Japan and Korea, along with 16 top domestic plastic surgery experts, delivering hands-on teaching through comprehensive video surgical demonstrations covering everything from basic theory to advanced repair.

14th Chang Gung Aesthetics Conference

Conference Dates Sep 27-28, 2025
Venue Linkou CGMH
International Speakers Japan, Korea
Domestic Speakers 16 Experts

Core Topics

  • Blepharoplasty: Upper and lower eyelid aesthetic surgery
  • Ptosis Correction: Functional and aesthetic levator muscle surgery
  • Canthoplasty: Medial and lateral canthal adjustments for eye shape optimization
  • Pretarsal Fullness: Restoring youthful aegyo-sal appearance
  • Lower Eyelid Lowering: Advanced techniques for specific aesthetic goals
  • Revisional Surgery: Secondary surgery, complication management, and case rescue
  • AI-Assisted Technology: Artificial intelligence for preoperative assessment and outcome simulation
