2026 Master Course: Deep Plane Facelift × Upper Blepharoplasty × Threadlift

November 1, 2026 (Sun) 08:00–17:00 Chang Gung Surgical Training Academy & Research Center (STARC) Education & Training

Course Overview

The Chang Gung Surgical Training Academy & Research Center (STARC) is pleased to present the 2026 Advanced Facial Rejuvenation Master Course, to be held on Sunday, November 1, 2026, from 08:00 to 17:00 as a cadaveric course.

The course focuses on three core subjects — Deep Plane Facelift, Upper Blepharoplasty, and Threadlift — taught by a faculty of master surgeons from Taiwan and abroad, with the choice of hands-on cadaver dissection or observation. It offers a rare opportunity for advanced training in aesthetic facial surgery.
