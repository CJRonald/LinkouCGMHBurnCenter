2026 Chang Gung Aesthetics Conference: Mastering the Art of Facelift

October 30-31, 2026 International Conference Hall, Research Building, Linkou CGMH Academic Events

Save the Date

The Department of Plastic Surgery, Linkou Chang Gung Memorial Hospital is delighted to announce the 2026 Chang Gung Aesthetics Conference (CGAC 2026), to be held on October 30-31, 2026 at the International Conference Hall, Research Building, Chang Gung Memorial Hospital, Linkou, Taiwan.

Themed "Mastering the Art of Facelift: From Lifting to Rejuvenation," the conference brings together leading experts in aesthetic plastic surgery to explore the latest advances in facelift surgery — from anatomy-based strategies and SMAS/deep plane repositioning to endoscopic forehead and midface lifting, with live surgical video demonstrations throughout.

CGAC 2026 — Mastering the Art of Facelift

Date Oct 30-31, 2026
Venue International Conference Hall, Linkou
Faculty Taiwan & Abroad
Format Surgical Video Demos

Program Highlights

  • Anatomy-Based Facelift Strategies
  • SMAS & Deep Plane Repositioning
  • Endoscopic Forehead & Midface Lift
  • Facelift vs. Thread Lift Approaches
  • Surgical Video Demonstrations
  • Facial Rejuvenation Concepts & Innovations

Venue & Access

Venue: International Conference Hall, 1st Floor, Research Building, Linkou CGMH
Address: No. 5, Fuxing St., Guishan Dist., Taoyuan City, Taiwan
Access: Adjacent to Chang Gung Memorial Hospital Station (A8) on the Taoyuan Airport MRT
