  • 21-bed critical care isolation unit
  • Professional medical team care
  • Advanced wound assessment technology
  • Critical monitoring and life support

For burn sequelae and chronic scars, we provide personalized reconstructive surgery, laser therapy, and rehabilitation programs to help patients improve appearance and function.

Treatment Methods

Conservative Treatment

  • Intralesional injection therapy
  • Cryotherapy
  • Laser scar treatment
  • Pressure therapy and rehabilitation

Surgical Treatment

  • Scar excision and reconstruction surgery
  • Skin grafting surgery
  • Flap transplantation surgery
