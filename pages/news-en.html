January 27, 2026 Memorial Event

10th Anniversary of Formosa Fun Coast Dust Explosion & Memorial Service for Dr. Hsiu-Shu Chuang

Marking the 10th anniversary of the Formosa Fun Coast dust explosion, Linkou CGMH held a memorial event on January 27th themed 'Gratitude, Hope, and Moving Forward.' During the disaster, Linkou CGMH received over 40 patients within four hours, performed more than 200 surgeries during the acute phase, and achieved a survival rate of over 95%. The event specially commemorated Dr. Hsiu-Shu Chuang, who passed away in December. Singer Selina also recorded a video to thank the medical team and pay tribute to all survivors.

January 15, 2025 Research

AI Burn Diagnosis System Officially Implemented in Clinical Practice

Our self-developed vision-driven intelligent burn analysis and prediction system, after two years of clinical trial validation with diagnostic accuracy exceeding 95%, has been officially implemented in daily clinical diagnostic procedures. This system not only improves diagnostic efficiency but also provides strong diagnostic support for young physicians, marking the entry of burn medicine into a new era of AI-assisted diagnosis.

January 8, 2025 Medical Services

New 3D Printed Personalized Reconstruction Services

To provide more precise personalized medical services, our center officially launched 3D printed personalized reconstruction technology, particularly for nasal reconstruction surgery. Through high-precision 3D scanning and modeling technology, we create customized surgical guidance templates for each patient, significantly improving surgical precision and success rates while reducing recovery time.

September 10, 2024 International Exchange

International Burn Reconstruction Expert Dr. Mark Fisher Visits Linkou CGMH

Prof. Mark Fisher from Johns Hopkins Hospital, Chair of the Reconstruction Committee of the American Burn Association, visited Linkou CGMH Burn Center for an in-depth academic exchange. Dr. Fisher shared his expertise on free flap surgery in burn reconstruction and highly praised our center's microsurgical flap achievements, recognizing it as a leading standard in burn care across the Asia-Pacific region.

December 20, 2024 Research

Customized Extensor Reconstruction Technology Receives International Patent Certification

Our center's developed "Customized Extensor Mechanism Reconstruction System" has received patent certification from the United States, European Union, and Japan. This innovative technology combines precision mechanical design with biomechanical analysis, providing personalized treatment solutions for patients with hand dysfunction. Currently, 15 patients have received treatment, with hand function recovery rates exceeding 85%.
