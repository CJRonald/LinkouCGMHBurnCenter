About Us

Since its establishment in 1978, Linkou CGMH Burn Center has become Taiwan's largest and most well-equipped burn center

Professional Medical Team

Founded by Dr. Samuel Noordhoff, our center now features 30 specialized beds, including 21 acute care intensive isolation beds and 9 sub-acute burn beds. We are equipped with 2 dedicated operating rooms, hydrotherapy rooms, silicone sand beds, and air mattresses.

We hold regular interdisciplinary team meetings weekly, bringing together physicians, nurses, nutritionists, physical therapists, occupational therapists, respiratory therapists, and social workers to provide comprehensive medical care services.

Center Features

  • Taiwan's largest burn center
  • 24-hour professional medical team
  • Interdisciplinary integrated burn care
  • Innovative clinical research

Services

Four specialties providing comprehensive burn care

Critical Burn Care

24-hour dedicated medical team with advanced ICU and emergency treatment facilities, providing immediate high-quality critical care from field rescue to hospitalization.

Chronic Scar Reconstruction

Personalized reconstruction surgery, laser therapy, and rehabilitation programs for burn sequelae and chronic scars, helping patients improve appearance and function.

Facial Plastic Reconstruction

Specializing in eye and nasal plastic reconstruction, combining precise surgical techniques with aesthetic design to help patients restore facial function and natural appearance.

Hand Surgery & Joint Reconstruction

Providing microsurgery, joint reconstruction, and functional rehabilitation for hand burns, trauma, or joint contractures, helping patients restore hand mobility.

Research

Combining AI technology with clinical experience to advance burn medicine innovation

Vision-Driven Intelligent Burn Analysis

Utilizing artificial intelligence and medical imaging analysis to develop automated burn area and depth recognition systems, improving clinical diagnostic accuracy and efficiency.

3D Printed Personalized Reconstruction

Combining 3D printing technology to design customized reconstruction solutions, particularly for nasal reconstruction, providing precise personalized medical services.

Customized Extensor Reconstruction

Developing innovative hand function reconstruction techniques, providing customized devices and surgical solutions for extensor dysfunction.
